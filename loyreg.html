Loyalty Schemes

Basic Loyalty Scheme

  1. Points Accumulation: Customers earn points for every purchase, which they can later redeem for discounts or free products/services.

  2. Exclusive Offers: Access to special discounts, promotions, or coupons available only to loyalty program members.

  3. Birthday Rewards: Special offers or discounts on the customer's birthday as a gesture of appreciation.

  4. Early Access:Priority access to new products, services, or sales events before they are available to the general public.



Premium Loyalty Scheme

  1. Enhanced Rewards: More generous rewards, such as higher point multipliers or bigger discounts on purchases.

  2. Dedicated Support: Access to dedicated customer support representatives for quicker assistance and issue resolution.

  3. Extended Benefits: Additional perks like free shipping, extended warranty, or complimentary services.

  4. Personalized Offers: Tailored promotions and offers based on the customer's purchase history and preferences.



VIP Loyalty Scheme

  1. Elite Status: Recognition as a valued customer with exclusive VIP status.

  2. Premium Gifts: Special gifts or luxury items offered as rewards for loyalty and continued patronage.

  3. Priority Service Expedited service and priority treatment in-store or online, including dedicated checkout lanes or priority customer service hotlines.

  4. VIP Events: Invitation to high-profile events, celebrity meet-and-greets, or luxury travel experiences exclusively for VIP members.

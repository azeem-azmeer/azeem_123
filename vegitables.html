Our suppliers

Local Farms Cooperative

The Local Farms Cooperative is a network of small to medium-sized farms located within a 50-mile radius of the city. These farms specialize in sustainable and organic farming practices, ensuring the freshest and highest quality produce.



Wholesale Market Exchange

The Wholesale Market Exchange is a bustling marketplace located on the outskirts of the city, where farmers, distributors, and supermarket buyers converge to trade produce. Spanning several acres, this venue hosts a plethora of stalls and warehouses stocked with vegetables sourced from local and regional farms, as well as international suppliers.



Vertical Farming Co-op

The Vertical Farming Co-op is revolutionizing the way vegetables are grown and distributed in urban areas. Situated in repurposed warehouses within the city limits, this innovative cooperative utilizes cutting-edge hydroponic and vertical farming techniques.

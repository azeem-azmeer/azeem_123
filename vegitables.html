Our suppliers

Local Farms Cooperative


The Local Farms Cooperative is a network of small to medium-sized farms located within a 50-mile radius of the city. These farms specialize in sustainable and organic farming practices, ensuring the freshest and highest quality produce. The cooperative operates a central distribution hub where member farms deliver their freshly harvested vegetables daily. Here, supermarket representatives can select from a wide variety of seasonal vegetables, ranging from leafy greens and root vegetables to exotic heirloom varieties.




Wholesale Market Exchange


The Wholesale Market Exchange is a bustling marketplace located on the outskirts of the city, where farmers, distributors, and supermarket buyers converge to trade produce. Spanning several acres, this venue hosts a plethora of stalls and warehouses stocked with vegetables sourced from local and regional farms, as well as international suppliers. Here, supermarket buyers can negotiate directly with farmers and distributors to secure bulk quantities of a wide range of vegetables at competitive prices.




Vertical Farming Co-op


The Vertical Farming Co-op is revolutionizing the way vegetables are grown and distributed in urban areas. Situated in repurposed warehouses within the city limits, this innovative cooperative utilizes cutting-edge hydroponic and vertical farming techniques to produce a wide variety of leafy greens, herbs, and other vegetables in a controlled indoor environment. With stacked growing beds and advanced LED lighting systems, the co-op is able to maximize space and efficiency, yielding bountiful harvests year-round.
